Share a little, Care a little - Donate Blood

It takes only15 minutesto donate blood!!!

The term BLOOD DONATION though consist of 13 letters, but the beautiful essence of saving lives behind it, equals million letters. It is a wonderful contribution to share a part of your belonging to the one who needs it.

 FOR BETTER EXPERIENCE

HYDRATE


Day before...

Increase your fluids the day before donating.

SNACKS



Evening before...

Eat salty snacks to increase salt intake the evening before donating.

SLEEP





Night before...

Get a good night's sleep prior to donation day.

BREAKFAST




Morning...

Eat a well balanced breakfast the morning of your donation.

SCREENING



Before donation...

Get yourself checked to find if you are eligible for blood donation.

REFRESHMENTS



After donation...

Eat snacks and drink something in refreshment area. Enjoy the feeling of helping someone.
